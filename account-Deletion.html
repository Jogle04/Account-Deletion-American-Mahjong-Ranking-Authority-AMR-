Account Deletion — American Mahjong Ranking Authority (AMR)

Users can delete their AMR account at any time directly within the app.

How to delete your account

  • Open the AMR app
  • Navigate to MyAMR
  • Tap Settings (top right corner)
  • Scroll down to the Danger Zone
  • Select Delete Account and confirm

What happens when you delete your account

  • Your account is permanently deleted
  • All associated personal data is erased from our systems
  • This action cannot be undone
